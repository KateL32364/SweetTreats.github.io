About Us

Our Story

We are a small bakery in the heart of a small town, creating the sweetest treats anyone can imagine! For over 10 years, we have poured our hearts into crafting delicious pastries, cakes, cookies, and specialty desserts. We work hard every day to ensure your cravings are never left unsatisfied.

Our Bakery

Meet Our Bakers

Chelsea

Chef

Meet Chelsea, our chef. She is the main baker and has studied at Culinary Arts of America. She settled into the small bakery after starting her family and wanted to share her gifts with the world in the form of sweet treats

Kenneth

Sous Chef

Meet Kenneth, our sous chef. He's been baking since he was a young man and has travelled all over the world and found the best ingredients to make the best treats.

Sarah

Apprentice

Meet Sarah, our newest addition. She had started studies at the Institute of Culinary Education, but had to return home to help take care of her parents. She now studies under Chelsea and hopes to help take Sweet Treats to new heights.
